-- About Us --

We started this as a way for pet owners to manage their pet's life and ensure their safety and health. We realised that many pet owners including ourselves were struggling to find the best vets at key times. We also saw alot of pets getting lost and their gps tracker app wouldnt function. Lastly we wanted pets to make friends and be engaged. Which is why we came up with Happy Paws.
